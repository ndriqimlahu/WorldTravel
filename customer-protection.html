We care about your safety

Stay safely with us, because we strive to help everyone experience the world safely. We have many processes in place to protect our guests, and to empower you to take control of your safety while you travel. Visit our traveller resource page to learn more about making your future stays go smoothly.

Host safely by bringing peace of mind to our World Travel. Partners is a top priority. We help hosts feel confident about welcoming guests, and have processes in place to protect both you and your property. You can learn more about how to stay safe while hosting on our Partner resource page.

In the unlikely event that something goes wrong, we are here for you. In this section, you can find guidelines to follow should an issue arise, as well as the steps we will take to look after you.
